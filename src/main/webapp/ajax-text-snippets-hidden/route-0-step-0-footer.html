Your Australian Government Service number can be found on your last payslip. If your AGS number is not on your last payslip, then please see the last Member Statement you received or contact your Personnel Officer.

Your Australian Defence Force number is your service prefix (A, R, N) followed by your PM Keys.

Please note that if you have an associate membership created as a result of a Family Law settlement, your AGS number or Service Number can be found on the Payment Split Notice sent to you following the superannuation split.
